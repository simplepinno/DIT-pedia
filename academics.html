Academics

Competence-based technical education for Tanzania's industrialization

Academic Overview

Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology offers competence-based technical education across multiple disciplines. Our programs are designed to equip students with practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and entrepreneurial mindset to drive Tanzania's industrialization agenda.

All programs are accredited by the National Council for Technical Education (NACTE) and approved by the Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU) for degree programs.
