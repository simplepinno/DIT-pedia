📚 Entry Requirements

Minimum qualifications for admission to DIT programs

Ordinary Diploma

NTA Level 4–6 | 2–3 years

Direct Entry (CSEE):

  • Certificate of Secondary Education (CSEE) with at least four (4) passes (D grade or higher) in:
    • Physics / Engineering Science
    • Mathematics
    • Chemistry
    • Any other subject (excluding religious subjects)

Alternative Entry:

  • General Certificate Course in Engineering (GCE), OR
  • CSEE with minimum D grade in Mathematics + NVA Level III or Trade Test Certificate Grade I in relevant field from VETA accredited institution

Special Note: For Diploma in Science and Laboratory Technology, Biomedical Equipment Engineering, and Food Science and Technology — CSEE with D grade or higher in relevant subjects is required.

Bachelor Degree — 3 Years

NTA Level 7–8 | 3 years

Option 1 — Diploma Holders:

  • Ordinary Diploma (NTA Level 6) or equivalent in the respective field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 from a recognized institution
  • At least four (4) passes (D grade or higher) in relevant subjects at CSEE / GCE / NVA Level III / Trade Test Grade I
  • Minimum D grade in Mathematics at CSEE

Option 2 — FTC Holders:

  • Full Technician Certificate (FTC) or equivalent in the relevant field
  • Average of minimum pass of C or average of minimum 3 points (A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2)
  • At least four (4) passes (D grade or higher) in relevant subjects at CSEE / GCE / Trade Test Grade I
  • Minimum D grade in Mathematics at CSEE

Bachelor Degree — 4 Years

NTA Level 7–8 | 4 years

Option 1 — ACSEE Holders:

  • Advanced Certificate of Secondary Education (ACSEE) in:
    • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM), OR
    • Physics, Geography, and Mathematics (PGM)
  • Principal Pass in Mathematics and Physics from the same sitting
  • Total of not less than 4.0 points

Conversion Scale:

  • Pre-2014 & Post-2015: A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1, S=0.5, F=0
  • 2014 & 2015: A=5, B+=4, B=3, C=2, D=1, E=0.5, F=0

Option 2 — NTA Level 6 Holders:

  • Applicants with NTA Level 6 qualifications applying for a different field of study must also meet the requirements stated above

Postgraduate Programs

Master's Degree | 18 months – 2 years

Master of Engineering in Maintenance Management

  • Bachelor Degree in Engineering with GPA of at least 2.7, OR
  • Bachelor Degree in Engineering with PASS + 3 years working experience, OR
  • Advanced Diploma in Engineering with PASS + 3 years working experience

Master of Technology in Computing and Communications

  • Bachelor Degree in Engineering or Science with GPA of at least 2.7, OR
  • Bachelor Degree in Engineering or Science with PASS + 'B' grade or higher in subjects related to the programme, OR
  • Advanced Diploma in Engineering or Science with PASS + 5 years working experience

📌 Other Postgraduate Programs: Master of Engineering in Sustainable Energy Engineering, Master in Computational Science and Engineering, Master of Science in Cyber Security and Digital Forensic, Master of Information Systems Engineering and Management, and Master of Engineering in Telecommunication Systems and Networks — similar requirements apply. Contact the admissions office for specific details.
