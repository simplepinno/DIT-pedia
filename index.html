History of Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology (DIT)

Introduction

Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology (DIT) is one of Tanzania's leading higher learning institutions specializing in engineering, science, and technology. It is located in Dar es Salaam and is committed to providing quality education, research, and innovation.

History

DIT was established in 1997 by an Act of Parliament to replace the Technical College, which had been operating since 1957. The institution was created to provide technical education and produce highly skilled technicians and engineers to support Tanzania's industrial and technological development.

Over the years, DIT has expanded its academic programs from diploma courses to bachelor's and postgraduate degrees in various fields of engineering, information technology, and applied sciences. The institute continues to improve its facilities and strengthen partnerships with industries to ensure students receive practical and modern technical education.

Mission

DIT aims to provide high-quality competency-based education, research, consultancy, and innovation that contribute to sustainable national and global development.

Vision

To become a leading centre of excellence in engineering, technology, and innovation in Africa.
