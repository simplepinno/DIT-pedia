Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology

An Agent of Industrialization. A Progressive and Customer-Centered Higher Learning Institution.

Established by Act of Parliament No. 6 of 1997

Learn More

Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology (DIT) is a leading public technical institution in Tanzania. Established in 1957 as the Dar es Salaam Technical Institute, DIT has evolved into a premier center for competence-based technical education, research, consultancy, and innovation.

Our Campuses

DIT has four main campuses across Tanzania

Main Campus

Location: Junction of Morogoro Road and Bibi Titi Mohamed Street, Dar es Salaam

Focus: The central hub housing the institute's administration. Offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs including Civil, Computer, Electrical, Mechanical, and Electronics & Telecommunications.

Mwanza Campus

Location: Mwanza Region

Focus: Specializes in technical and vocational programs with a strong regional focus on Laboratory Science and Leather Technology.

Visit Mwanza Campus →

Songwe Campus (Myunga)

Location: Songwe Region

Focus: Offers technical and vocational skills training including Civil Engineering, Plumbing, and ICT.

Visit Songwe Campus →

Dodoma Campus

Location: Dodoma Region

Focus: Dedicated to skills development for secondary school graduates transitioning from vocational training centers.
